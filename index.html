The Phantom Lighthouse

At the edge of a rugged coast, where the cliffs meet the relentless sea, stood the abandoned Garrison Lighthouse. For decades, the lighthouse had been out of service, its beam extinguished and its walls weathered by salt and wind. Locals avoided it, whispering tales of eerie lights and ghostly apparitions that would appear on foggy nights. Few dared to venture close, but curiosity had a way of drawing people in.
